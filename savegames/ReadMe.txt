## Savegame for TRR 1 2 3

The savegame.TR123.Unlocked.zip enables all levels in challenge mode
All Hallows and Vegas included.

The savegame.TR123.Challenge.zip enables all levels in challenge mode
and contains levels at different states.

## Savegame for TRR 4 5 6

Backup your savegame.dat.

Rename the savegame.ext to savegame.dat and copy it in 
C:\Users\{YourUsername}\AppData\Roaming\TRX2\{YourID}

Then launch TRR 4 5 6.

Note that I custom Keys so change it if your want.
View 	: Num 0
Flares 	: Num 5
Duck 	: Num 1
Dash 	: Num 3

savegame.TRR4.zip contains all levels of TRR 4 (Last Revelation)
savegame.TRR6.zip contains all levels of TRR 6 (Angel Of Darkness)
